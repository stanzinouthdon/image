Skyu - A Traditional Ladakhi Dish

Skyu is a common pasta dish in Ladakhi villages, made with handmade flour pasta, local herbs, spices, and seasonal vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Goat milk
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Onion & Tomato (for sauce)
  • Salt & Spices (as required)

Instructions

  1. Prepare a dough using wheat flour, baking soda, and water.
  2. Roll the dough into long thin tubes. Pinch pieces and press them with your thumb.
  3. Stir-fry onion, tomato, and other vegetables. Add spices as required.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil. Release the Skyu pasta into the boiling water.
  5. Let it boil for 10 minutes, then add goat milk.
  6. Simmer for a few more minutes until creamy.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your traditional Ladakhi Skyu!
