Thukpa - Traditional Ladakhi Noodle Soup

Thukpa is a traditional Ladakhi noodle soup, packed with vegetables, spices, and handmade pasta. A warm and hearty dish perfect for cold weather!

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves (dhania patta)
  • 1/4 cup spring onion greens, chopped
  • 3 radishes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or as needed)
  • 4 cups water or vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan. Add onions and garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  3. Mix in cumin powder, garam masala, and black pepper. Stir well.
  4. Add radish and other chopped vegetables. Stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in 4 cups of water (or vegetable broth) and bring to a boil.
  6. Meanwhile, knead wheat flour dough and roll it into thin sheets.
  7. Cut the dough into bite-sized irregular pieces and drop them into the simmering broth.
  8. Let it cook for 10 minutes until the noodles are soft.
  9. Add salt, lemon juice, and garnish with coriander and spring onion greens.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your Ladakhi Thukpa!
