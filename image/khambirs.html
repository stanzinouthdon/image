Khambir - Traditional Ladakhi Bread

Khambir is a pan-shaped Ladakhi bread with a thick crust and soft inside, made from local whole wheat. It is commonly served for breakfast with butter tea or salt tea.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Pul (local baking soda from Nubra Valley)
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough using wheat flour, baking soda, and water. Knead until soft.
  2. Traditionally, it is cooked on a cast iron plate over a wood or cow dung stove.
  3. To enhance crispiness, it is finished off on embers inside the stove.
  4. Serve hot with butter and apricot jam.
